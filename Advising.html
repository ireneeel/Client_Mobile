UMSI Advising

Welcome to UMSI Advising

Our advising services at UMSI are designed to support students throughout their academic journey. Whether you’re seeking guidance on course selection, career planning, or finding balance between school and personal life, our team is here to assist you every step of the way.

Advising Services

We offer a wide range of advising services to help students succeed academically and personally:

  • **Academic Planning**: Get help with choosing the right courses and creating a roadmap to graduation.
  • **Career Advising**: Discuss career goals, internship opportunities, and job placements with experienced advisors.
  • **Personal Support**: Find resources to manage stress, improve time management, and balance academic and personal life.

Meet Our Advisors

Our advising team consists of experienced professionals dedicated to student success. Advisors are available by appointment and during walk-in hours. You can schedule a meeting with your assigned advisor or visit during open office hours for quick questions.

How to Schedule an Appointment

To schedule an advising appointment, follow these simple steps:

  • Log in to the student portal and navigate to the Advising section.
  • Choose your advisor and select an available time slot.
  • Receive a confirmation email with your appointment details.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to contact the UMSI advising office at advising@umsi.edu. We’re here to help!
